\paragraph{Verb Prefixes in Sesotho}

Most common:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Subject agreement (sm or sr)
    \item Tense/aspect marker (t{\^\ })
    \item Object agreement (om)
\end{enumerate}



Other morphemes
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Subject agreement
    \item Copula (cp)
    \item Negation
    \item Tense/aspect marker
    \item conditional morpheme (\emph{ho}, `if')
    \item Reflexive (rf)
    \item Object agreement
\end{enumerate}
